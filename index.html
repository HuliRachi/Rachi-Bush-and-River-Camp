A Nature Camp For
Serious Nature Lovers

Located in the stunningly beautiful Niani region of the Limpopo Province. Rachi bush and River camp has one of the best climates in the world, with mild winters and temperate summers it can be visited all year round with easy access from nearby cities (Musina only 2hours drive) and (Thohoyandou only 5hours drive) just off R525 between Nthakheni and Makuya park.

What To Expect

The bush camp is home to approximately 60 large mammal and roughly 400 bird species. You will find all these in an area comprising vast open plains of grassland , woodlands, rocky outcrops and mountains.

The big 5 are presented in the campsile and you are likey to spot lion, leopard, elephant, rhino or buffal. During your visit you will also have the rare privillage of possibly seeing highly endangered.
